Facilities &

Services

Parking Lots

Dawood Market Hyderabad provides spacious parking facilities for customers, ensuring a hassle-free experience. Designated parking areas for both cars and bikes are available, with clear guidance and organized layouts for smooth traffic flow.

Security

The mall ensures 24-hour security both inside and outside the premises. A combination of trained security personnel and CCTV surveillance systems guarantees a safe and secure environment for all visitors.

Mother's Room

Experience comfort and convenience at Dawood Market with our Mother's Room – a private, tranquil space equipped with amenities for nursing mothers. Located on the 2nd floor, it offers a relaxing environment for mothers and their infants.

Information Desk

The Information Desk at Dawood Mart Hyderabad is here to assist you. Whether you need help finding a store, have questions about our services, or require any support during your visit, our friendly staff is always ready to help. Located conveniently near the main entrance, the desk provides guidance and ensures your shopping experience is smooth and enjoyable.

ATM

For your convenience, Dawood Mart Hyderabad provides ATM services within the mall premises. Whether you need cash withdrawals or other banking services, the ATMs are easily accessible and located in key areas to ensure a hassle-free shopping experience.

Wheelchair Accessibility

Dawood Mart Hyderabad is committed to being inclusive for everyone. The mall is equipped with wheelchair-accessible ramps, elevators, and wide pathways to ensure a comfortable and seamless shopping experience for individuals with mobility challenges. Wheelchairs are also available upon request at the Information Desk.

Kids' Play Area

Dawood Mart Hyderabad features a dedicated Kids' Play Area, designed to keep young ones entertained while parents shop. This safe and fun space is equipped with age-appropriate games, slides, and activities, providing a joyful experience for children and peace of mind for parents.

Prayer Rooms

Dawood Mart Hyderabad offers dedicated Prayer Rooms for both men and women, providing a peaceful and private space for religious practices. These clean and serene rooms are conveniently located within the mall to ensure comfort and accessibility for all visitors.

Medical Assistance

Dawood Mart Hyderabad is committed to ensuring the well-being of all its visitors. We provide Medical Assistance with a first aid station located within the mall. In case of an emergency, our trained staff is ready to offer immediate help, and we also have access to nearby medical facilities for further care if needed.
